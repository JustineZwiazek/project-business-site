FAQ

Why rent with us?

Upcycled clothing uses significantly fewer raw materials, reducing the harmful impacts of fashion production, and avoiding contributing to landfill.

Can you shop online?

We don't currently run an online store, you are very welcome to visit us at one of our three shops in Stockholm

Where are Ambry clothing produced?

We source our garments from European second-hand wholesellers and upcycle them on location, in Stockholm. Any items not used get sent back to our suppliers.
